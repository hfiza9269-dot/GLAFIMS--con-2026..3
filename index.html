About The Conference

Integrating Education, Ethics & Innovation for Tomorrow's Justice

Advancing Forensic Science Through Innovation

The GLAFIMS Forensic Workshop and International Conference 2026 brings together leading professionals, researchers, and students in the field of forensic medicine and investigative sciences.

This premier event will feature cutting-edge research presentations, hands-on workshops, and networking opportunities with international experts. Our goal is to foster collaboration and advance the field through knowledge sharing and innovation.

With topics ranging from digital forensics to forensic pathology, this conference offers something for every professional in the field.

Travel Information

NSC Government Medical College is located in Khandwa, Madhya Pradesh, with excellent connectivity via road, rail, and air.

By Train

Khandwa Railway Station is well connected to major cities across India with regular trains from Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata, and Chennai.

By Bus

Regular bus services from Indore, Bhopal, and other major cities. Luxury AC buses available for comfortable travel.

By Air

Nearest airport: Devi Ahilya Bai Holkar Airport, Indore (100 km). Direct flights from major Indian cities. Airport transfer can be arranged.
